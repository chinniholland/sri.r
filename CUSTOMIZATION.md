### 2. Featured Repositories

**How to Pin Repositories:**
1. Go to your GitHub profile
2. Click "Customize your pins"
3. Select up to 6 repositories to feature
4. Prioritize:
   - Most impressive projects
   - Well-documented code
   - Active/maintained repos
   - Projects relevant to target roles

## 💡 Pro Tips for Profile Success

### ✨ Stand Out
1. **Consistency** - Maintain code quality across projects
2. **Documentation** - Clear, comprehensive READMEs
3. **Activity** - Regular commits show dedication
4. **Quality** - Better to have 5 great projects than 50 mediocre
5. **Personality** - Let your unique style shine

### 🎯 Target Audience Appeal
- **Employers** - Clean, professional, skills-focused
- **Collaborators** - Active, well-documented, community-engaged
- **Learners** - Tutorials, examples, clear explanations
- **Tech Community** - Innovation, contribution, mentorship

### 📈 Growth Strategy
- Star quality > quantity of repositories
- Regular activity over sporadic bursts
- Documentation > bare code
- Contribution > competition
- Community > isolation
